Welcome to Galaxy

A galaxy y Way is a gravitationally bound system of stars , star clusters , interstellar gas and dust , dark matter , and planets . All objects in a galaxy are involved in motion relative to a common center of mass .All galaxies are extremely distant astronomical objects . The distance to the nearest ones is measured in megaparsecs , and to the most distant ones - in units of redshift z .
